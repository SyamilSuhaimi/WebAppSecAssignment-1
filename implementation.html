The login form earlier when trying to access this web was created to showcase access control and validation.

Access control and validation are crucial components of data security and are essential for protecting sensitive information and ensuring that only authorized individuals that have appropriate access. Access control and validation are crucial for ensuring data security, preventing unauthorized access, meeting regulatory requirements, mitigating risks, and safeguarding sensitive information. By implementing robust access control measures, organizations can protect their assets and maintain the confidentiality, integrity, and availability of their data.
